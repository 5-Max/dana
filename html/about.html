Welcome!

My name is Dana. I trained at Van Michael salons. Worked there as a stylist for ten years before deciding to take my show on the road. I specialize in all hair cuts for men, women, and children. Families can save time and money by booking a salon quality haircut in the privacy of their home. My hours are very flexible and you won't have to wait weeks for an appointment. I also have a studio in my home for those who prefer to come to me.

Please text(preferred), email, or call for an appointment!
